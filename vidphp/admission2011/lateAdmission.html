Welcome to Vidyalaya Registration process (late) for 2011-12. Vidyalaya is an all-volunteer organization and we look forward to working with you to provide a good learning environment for our families. More information about our process is available at http://www.vidyalaya.us/modx/129. This page also contains frequently asked questions. Please reply to this email for any issue relating to registration process.

To be eligible for enrollment in 2011-12, a student should be enrolled in KG through 12 grades in the Fall of 2011 at their regular school. This email is being sent to the parents who did not register in time but want to join school for 2011-12. This year we are doing it without any late fees.

Following documents are attached

  1. Family Registration Form - Please verify information and provide any missing contact information
  2. Volunteer Form - Volunteering is a requirement for all families. Please select what you would like to volunteer for and put the codes in the registration form
  3. Participation Agreement - Please read the terms of your participation at Vidyalaya
  4. Layout - Layout of the school, please print one copy per eligible student

We request you to complete the following steps

  1. Read all the information attached and learn more about us at http://www.vidyalaya.us.
  2. Please come to Parsippany Hills High School, 20 Rita Drive, Morris Plains, NJ 07950, (Directions) Sunday October 2, 2011 at 9:15 am.
  3. Please confirm today (October 1, 2011) that you are coming.
  4. Bring completed registration form (one per family), Medical Information form (one per child).
  5. New Student fee of $50 applies to any student not registered in 2010-11. Prayer CD, School Bag and Prayer Book provided provided to all new students.
  6. Make check payable to Vidyalaya Inc with correct fee.
  7. Write your family ID on the check.
  8. Sign and date all pages returned.
  9. Please do not forget to fill in the volunteering codes in the registration form.

The Kindergarten students can sit in the prayer session after the registration is complete. They will need to go Room D36 after prayers. KG students need to be signed in and out of the room.

Students, grade 1 and above, would need to be assesed for proper placement in the Language class. If you were enrolled last year, please bring your assessment report for faster processing. Once the language class is assigned, I will assign the culture class and let you know where you need to go. Please do come on time.
